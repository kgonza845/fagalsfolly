Public Comment – General City Business
10:20 Public Comment – Cynthia Farmer Speaks about widespread garbage issues, neglected vacant properties, and the City’s lack of visible cleanup efforts.

24:40 Public Comment – William Rivas Highlights a decade‑long community–police trust‑building model, including youth engagement, shared power in police hiring, and national recognition.
28:45 Public Comment – Marion Porterfield (former councilmember) Supports installing fencing to stop illegal dumping on Wiley Street and thanks volunteers and organizers for cleanup efforts. Thanks Housing Task Force Chair Kim Wiggins & One Schenectady for work to improve housing in Schenectady.
